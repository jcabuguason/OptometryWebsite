Comprehensive Eye Exam

Our Comprehensive Eye Exams are performed with state of the art optical equipment, including autorefractor, keratometer and electronic phoropter in order to enhance your experience, which helps us perform the eye exam to give you the most accurate prescription with minimal contact from our qualified optometrists. Retinal Images

We offer retinal images, which are pictures of the back of your eye. These images are key to maintaining your eye health, and can often lead to a early diagnosis of potential eye conditions that need to be addressed. Visual Fields

We offer visual fields testing which are used to determine if there are any blind spots or loss of vision which may occur and it can be used to monitor pre existing conditions. This is often required for RCMP, and police candidates, as well as for the ministry of transportation.
