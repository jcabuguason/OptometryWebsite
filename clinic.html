About Us

At I See Eyecare we strive to provide you the best services for your vision and eye health. We offer you Comprehensive Eye Exams, with state of the art atmosphere and optical equipment, our authorefracto/keratometer connected to our electronic phoropter help us to give you the must accurate prescription we can do, we offer you Retinal Imaging, non-contact Tonometry and Pachymetry that give us very good details of your ocular health without touching your face and keeping a good distance for your comfort and your safety! Visual Field Testing are available on site as well for patients that need them.

We have an array of designer frames (ophthalmic and sunglasses), high end lenses (Zeiss) and contact lenses. Our staff is extremely knowledgeable and will help advise you with all of your optical needs, including frame and prescription lens choices that best suit you.
